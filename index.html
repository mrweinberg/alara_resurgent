Alara Resurgent

The World

The Setting: It has been 30 years since the Conflux. The five shards have physically merged, but the psychological scars remain. The central conflict is no longer "Shard vs. Shard," but a civil war within each culture about how to adapt to a world with all five colors of mana.

The Central Conflict: Synthesis vs. Purity

* The Synthesists: Factions that embrace the new colors of mana flooding their lands.

* The Purists: Zealots who view the new mana as pollution and violently enforce old borders.

Mechanical Identities

  • Fabricate N (Esper - WUB): When this creature enters the battlefield, put N +1/+1 counters on it or create N 1/1 colorless Servo artifact creature tokens. (Restricted to Creatures/Vehicles).
  • Covenant (Bant - GWU): Abilities that are active or enhanced as long as you control a modified creature (a creature with a counter, Equipment, or Aura on it).
  • Exhume [Cost] (Grixis - UBR): You may cast this spell from your graveyard by paying [Cost] and exiling a creature card from your graveyard.
  • Carnage (Jund - BRG): Triggers whenever a permanent you control is put into a graveyard from the battlefield.
  • Titanic (Naya - RGW): Abilities that are active or enhanced if you control a creature with power 4 or greater.
  • Spectrum (5-Color): Triggers whenever you cast a multicolored spell.
