Alara Resurgent

The World

The Setting: It has been 30 years since the Conflux. The five shards have physically merged, but the psychological scars remain. The central conflict is no longer "Shard vs. Shard," but a civil war within each culture about how to adapt to a world with all five colors of mana.

The Central Conflict: Synthesis vs. Purity

* The Synthesists: Factions that embrace the new colors of mana flooding their lands.

* The Purists: Zealots who view the new mana as pollution and violently enforce old borders.

Mechanical Identities
